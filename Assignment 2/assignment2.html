In recent times, the world is polluted with more waste created by humans. It is important that we maintain protected areas as they allow for the ecosystem to continue the cycle of nature. As we speak, some animals face extinction, climate change becomes more apparent, and the ocean is tainted with human waste. It is because of the issues created by humans arise, that we must preserve them and do our part to keep our planet sustainable. Below are some links that will inform you of goals, issues we face and how we can play our part.

  • IUCN: International Union for Conservation of Nature
  • Protected Planet
  • IUCN Green List of Protected and Conserved Areas
  • IUCN Contributions for Nature
  • IUCN Red List of Threatened Species
  • ECOLEX: The Gateway to Environmental Law
